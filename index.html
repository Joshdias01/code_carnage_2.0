Code Carnage 2.0 is a 24-hour hackathon organized by the Data Science Department’s Numinous Technical Club, SJB Institute of Technology. It provides a platform for students to collaborate, innovate, and build technical solutions through coding.

The hackathon is open to college students from any discipline, including BE, BTech, or any other technical background. Anyone with an interest in coding, technology, and problem-solving is welcome to participate.

Yes. Inter-college teams are allowed, and participants from different institutions can form a team.

Code Carnage 2.0 is a team-based hackathon. Individual participation is not allowed. The team size is 2-4

The registration fee is ₹999 per team (not per participant).

On the first day, your food arrangements include lunch, dinner, and snacks, followed by breakfast, coffee, and tea on the second day.
